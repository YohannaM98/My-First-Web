Features

Smart Summaries

Your lectures, textbooks, or notes—condensed into crystal-clear takeaways. AI-Powered Parsing: Pulls out main points, keywords, and definitions. Study Modes: Toggle between quick-read, in-depth, or outline formats. Download or Print: Take them offline or turn them into printable cheat sheets. Searchable & Organized: Find what you need by topic, keyword, or course.

Quizzes and Practice exams

Test yourself with questions tailored to your content. Generated from Your Notes: Get MCQs, True/False, and short answers automatically. Timed & Untimed Modes: Simulate real exam settings or learn at your pace. Instant Feedback: Review right/wrong answers with clear explanations. Build Your Own Quiz: Select topics, question types, and difficulty.

Flashcards

Master key concepts with smart, AI-generated flashcards. Spaced Repetition: Automatically revisits concepts at optimal intervals to lock in memory. Tag & Organize: Sort cards by subject, topic, or difficulty. Quick Review Mode: Swipe through cards like you’re on a mission. Create Your Own: Customize or add new flashcards anytime.

Calendar & Study Planner

Keep track of everything—without falling behind. Personal Study Calendar: Plan your week, set goals, and block time. Exam Countdown: Know exactly how long you have left to prep. Progress Tracker: Watch your streak grow with visual progress bars. Smart Alerts: Get reminders for quizzes, flashcard reviews, or study sessions.
